WHY YOU SHOULD VISIT EGYPT

What you can do:

In Egypt, you can see iconic pyramids and temples like those at Giza and Luxor, explore the bustling cities of Cairo with its historic markets, cruise the ancient Nile River, discover vibrant marine life at Red Sea coral reefs, and explore serene oases and deserts.

Giza Plateau: Home to the Great Pyramid and the Great Sphinx, iconic symbols of ancient Egyptian civilization.

Nile River: Take a relaxing cruise along the lifeblood of Egypt, experiencing the timeless scenes along its banks.

Egyptian Museum: Located in Cairo, this museum houses the world's largest collection of Pharaonic antiquities, including treasures from Tutankhamun's tomb.

Things To Do in the Giza Plateau

Great Sphinx: This iconic monument has the body of a lion and the head of a pharaoh, likely Khafre.

Valley Temple: Located next to the Great Sphinx, this temple was used in the mummification process for King Khafre.

Great Pyramid of Giza (Pyramid of Khufu): The largest and oldest of the three main pyramids, it is the only remaining Wonder of the Ancient World.
